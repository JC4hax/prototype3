What is Granylst?

Granylst is a modern, responsive web app for creating, comparing, and analyzing data visually. AI-powered insights coming soon!

📈

Multiple Chart Types

Line, bar, pie, and scatter charts with smooth interactions and downloads.

⚡

Fast & Simple

Paste your data, choose a chart, and visualize instantly—no setup required.

🤖

AI Insights

Automatic summaries and statistics to highlight trends and anomalies.

🔁

Compare Datasets

Plot two datasets side-by-side to spot differences quickly.

Quick Start

  1. Open Create Graph and paste your data (or upload CSV).
  2. Select a chart type and generate your visualization.
  3. Download the chart or get AI insights for key takeaways.
